**📊 Superstore Performance Dashboard | Power BI
📌 Project Overview**

- The Superstore Performance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI analytics solution designed to provide a comprehensive view of business performance across sales, profit, customers, products, and regional operations.

- This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify business trends, evaluate product performance, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall profitability and operational efficiency.

**🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:**

- Monitor Sales & Profit Performance
- Track business growth trends
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products
- Evaluate regional and category-level performance
- Support strategic business decision making
- 📊 Dashboard Features
- Executive KPI Overview
- Total Sales Revenue
- Total Profit
- Order Quantity
- Customer Count
- Profit Margin %
- Average Order Value
- Sales Performance Analysis
- Sales Trend by Month / Year
- Revenue Growth Analysis
- Sales by Category & Sub-Category
- Regional Sales Comparison
- Profitability Insights
- Profit by Product Category
- Loss-Making Products Identification
- Category Contribution Analysis
- Profit Margin Tracking
- Customer & Product Analysis
- Top Customers by Revenue
- Customer Purchase Patterns
- Best Selling Products
- Product Performance Rankings
- Geographic Analysis
- Sales by Region / State
- Regional Profit Comparison
- Market Performance Insights
  
**🛠️ Tech Stack**
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX
- Power Query (M Language)
- Data Modeling
- Interactive Visualizations
